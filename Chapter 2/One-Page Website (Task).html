ABOUT

When eight friends are trapped on a remote mountain retreat and things quickly turn sinister, they start to suspect they aren’t alone.

Gripped by fear and with tensions in the group running high, you’ll be forced to make snap decisions that could mean life, or death, for everyone involved.

Every choice you make in your terrifying search for answers – even the seemingly trivial ones – will carve out your own unique story.

PLOT

"Until Dawn" centers around a group of eight friends who reunite at a secluded mountain lodge for a weekend getaway. However, their plans for relaxation turn into a nightmare when they become trapped on the mountain and hunted by a masked killer. As the night unfolds, the group discovers chilling secrets about the lodge's dark past, including the disappearance of two of their friends, Hannah and Beth, a year earlier. Haunted by guilt and regret, the remaining friends must confront their fears and work together to survive the night. Throughout their harrowing ordeal, players control the fate of each character through a series of choices and quick-time events. These decisions not only shape the characters' relationships but also determine who lives and who dies as they struggle to outwit the relentless killer. As tensions rise and alliances are tested, the true nature of the threat stalking them is revealed, leading to a heart-pounding climax where every decision counts. Only by making the right choices can players hope to see the survivors make it through until dawn and escape the mountain alive.

CHARACTERS

Samantha "Sam" Giddings

"You need to listen to me, I don't care if you believe me or not. Doesn't matter, because you will."

Diligent, considerate, and adventurous, Sam embodies levelheadedness in her group. She avoids conflict, caring deeply for animals. As Hannah's best friend, she tries to warn her about a prank, but is ignored. Afterward, she supports Hannah's brother, Josh.

Joshua "Josh" Washington

"Oh come, come-come-come-come. Why the long faces? Come on! It's good to get the heart racing every now and then, right?"

Josh, 20, a college dropout, is thoughtful and complex. As Hannah and Beth Washington's older brother, he's the group's joker. Guilty over his sisters' fate after a drunken prank, he later invites friends to honor their memory at the family lodge on the anniversary of their disappearance.

Emily "Em" Davis

"Rule number one, 'Emily is always right.' Rule number two, 'Nothing else matters because Emily is always right."

A college student with a perfect GPA known for her intelligence, resourcefulness, and sharp tongue. She values appearance and high-end fashion, often demanding the best. Despite her flaws, she was involved in the prank against Hannah. After splitting with Mike, she starts dating Matt.

Matthew "Matt" Taylor

"I'm not all muscle. There are some brains in here, too!"

A high school linebacker, Matt is motivated, ambitious, and athletic. While generally nice, he can seem like a pushover. He prioritizes his athleticism over intelligence. He filmed the prank against Hannah but didn't share it. He dates Emily after her breakup, but their relationship seems one-sided.

Jessica "Jess" Riley

"Who needs grades when you've got all the natural advantages you can handle?"

Jess, 18, a former homecoming queen, is confident, trusting, and irreverent. She's mischievous, fun-loving, and flirty, valuing her appearance highly and getting feisty when challenged. She orchestrated the prank on Hannah, citing it as protection for her friend Emily. Ironically, she's now with Mike, Emily's ex.

Michael "Mike" Munroe

"Come on guys, we're all friends here, right? No need for violence, just a little harmless fun! We're up in the woods! It's spooky! Come on, let's get into the spirit of things!"

Mike, a former class president, is known for his intelligence, determination, and charisma. He's a go-getter, always diving into challenges headfirst. Despite his charm, he's not immune to mistakes; he willingly takes part in the prank on Hannah. After breaking up with Emily, he dates Jessica.

Ashley "Ash" Brown

"You were looking on your phone, you dip! What are you tweeting!? Hashtag there's a freaking ghost after us?!"

Ashley, a bookworm, is academic, curious, and forthright. Often in the library, she's eager to learn. Despite her intelligence, her imagination can easily scare her. Though she played a minor role in the prank, she's usually the one investigating. Ashley crushes on Chris but doubts his feelings.

Christopher "Chris" Hartley

"Boom! Butterfly effect."

As a tech geek, Chris is methodical, protective, and humorous. He's the calmest in the group, often cracking jokes to lighten the mood. Best friends with Josh since grade school, he missed the prank on Hannah because he was drunk and asleep like Josh. Despite crushing on Ashley, he's too scared to advance their relationship.
